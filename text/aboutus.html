ABOUT US

Welcome to KRI! We're two students who created this platform to make life easier for other students. We know how challenging it can be to find the right place to live, especially when you're busy with studies. That's why we designed an easy-to-use website where students can quickly find rental options that match their needs and budget.

Our goal is simple: to make finding a place fast, safe, and accessible. We hope our platform helps you find the perfect spot for both your studies and everyday life.
